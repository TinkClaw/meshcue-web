The Platform

Three pillars. One mission.

Every clinic needs devices, communication, and resources. MeshCue connects all three into a single open platform.

Forge

AI hardware compiler. Describe a medical device in plain English, get circuits, firmware, 3D-printable enclosures, and docs.

  • ESP32, RP2040, STM32 support
  • KiCad PCB, Arduino firmware
  • OpenSCAD 3D enclosures
  • Medical device documentation (IEC 62304, FMEA)
  • Pulse oximeters, thermometers, BP monitors
Open Forge →

Connect

Patient messaging for clinics without internet. SMS, WhatsApp, USSD, and voice calls with auto-triage and fallback chains.

  • Africa's Talking, Twilio, Meta Cloud API
  • Auto-triage: vitals → priority → routing
  • 9 languages (EN, FR, SW, AR, PT...)
  • Consent management & HIPAA-aware logging
  • Exponential backoff with channel fallback
Explore Connect →

RAM

Resource & Asset Management. Track devices, medications, supply orders, and maintenance across every clinic.

  • Asset lifecycle tracking (register → maintain → retire)
  • Low-stock & expiry alerts
  • Supply chain ordering & fulfillment
  • Forge-built devices auto-registered
  • Per-clinic dashboard & analytics

How It Works

From description to deployed clinic

One platform handles the entire lifecycle: design, build, connect, manage.

1

Describe your device

"ESP32 pulse oximeter with OLED display for rural clinics, battery-powered, under $5 BOM"

2

Forge builds everything

Circuit diagram, Arduino firmware with real sensor calibration, 3D-printable enclosure, BOM, medical documentation — in seconds.

3

Connect links patients

Device alerts auto-triage to the right channel. Critical SpO2 reading? SMS the nurse immediately. Routine follow-up? WhatsApp in their language.

4

RAM tracks it all

Every device is auto-registered as an asset. Stock levels, maintenance schedules, supply orders — one dashboard per clinic.

The Mesh

Peer-to-peer healthcare network

Clinics form a mesh. Share compute, offload builds, relay messages when connectivity drops.

MeshCue Network

Nodes discover each other. Builds offload. Messages relay.
